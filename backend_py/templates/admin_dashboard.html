📖 About Swasthya

Swasthya is an AI-powered QR code based healthcare system designed to revolutionize patient care management. Our platform provides:

  • Instant Patient Identification: Quick QR code scanning for immediate access to medical records
  • Comprehensive Medical Records: Secure digital storage of patient history, prescriptions, and reports
  • Role-Based Security: Separate dashboards for Patients, Doctors, and Administrators
  • AI Health Insights: Intelligent analysis of patient data for better clinical decisions
  • Emergency Access: Critical information available without login during emergencies
  • Appointment Management: Efficient scheduling and tracking of patient visits
  • Paperless Operations: Complete digital workflow for modern healthcare
